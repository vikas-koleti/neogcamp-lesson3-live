What is Astrology?

The question might seem of little importance but astrology has been stuck in controversy since its arrival. There are people who accept astrology and also there are people who make fun of it. Astrology is primarily concerned with the planets, the sun, the moon, stars and stellar bodies inside and outside of the solar system. Vedic Astrology is based on stellar constellations. Vedas are very old scriptures.

Astrological Predictions are based on 9 Celestial bodies,12 Zodiac Signs, 27 star signs or Nakshatras, 12 houses.

Does astrology give accurate prediction?

There are many doubts about astrological prediction. Mathematicians and Scientists like Aryabhatta, Varaha mihira and Issac Newton were Astrolgers as well. Astrology combined with present day science and technology will definitely recover its ancient prestige.
